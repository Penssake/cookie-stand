Pat's Salmon Cookies

Seattleite Pat is a good guy. He's come up with Seattle's soon to be newest obsession. Pat has taken wild-caught salmon, chopped them up and baked right into his secret cookie dough recipe. Delicious! After years in the making you can now enjoy these fishy little treats at five locations throughout Seattle. Our newest location being located in Seattle's iconic Pike Place Market, right next to the well known Beecher's cheese.

Baker Buddy Biweekly

Every other Sunday starting 10.30.16

Join us for an all-ages cookie mingle! Bring the whole family and help the bakers of Pat's Salmon Cookies decorate the cookies for the week! Show up bright and early for the baking process at 4AM and experience the life of a baker. Following baking hour from 6AM to 8AM we will put the finishing touches on the cookies with the icing.

Best design wins a salmon shapped cookie cutter for their own at home baking projects!

As an added bonus, everyone will take home a bakers dozen to enjoy with the family! Please provide bakers with any food allergies or intolerances, they will be happy to accomodate you. Gluten free, Vegan, and dairy free options may require an additional fee.

Finally! Thank you to everyone who has supported Pat's cookies during these last two years. As we continue to grow we will continue to give back to the community that helps us thrive.
