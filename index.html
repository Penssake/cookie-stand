Pat's Salmon Cookies

Seattleite Pat is a good guy. He's come up with Seattle's soon to be newest obsession. Pat has taken wild-caught salmon, chopped them up and baked right into his secret cookie dough recipe. Delicious!

After years in the making you can now enjoy these fishy little treats in Seattle's iconic Pike Place Market, right next to the well known Beecher's cheese.
